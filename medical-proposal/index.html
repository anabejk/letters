Machine learning model predicts brain health
The new research led by Center for BrainHealth investigated neural biomarkers associated with improvements on a brain health index.
— 48 participants aged 21–65 completed a simple task;
— six months later they did it again;
— participants completed a battery of online cognitive assessments;
— a composite score of the brain’s holistic fitness, called the BrainHealth Index.
